# Privacy TL;DR

- We collect only what’s needed: account basics, precise GPS for matching, your short profile, trust signals, and app diagnostics.
- Messages are end‑to‑end encrypted. We cannot read them.
- Your real name/photo are hidden by default. You choose what to share and when.
- Intros (photo/voice) require consent before someone can view them.
- We use your data to match you, keep the community reliable, and run features you choose (chat, calendar).
- We don’t sell data, we don’t have an open user directory, and we don’t do ads tracking.
- Security: Signal‑compatible E2EE, encrypted storage, strict access controls.
- You control: language, theme, privacy toggles, download/delete data.
- Retention: minimal and time‑bound. You can delete your account and data.
- Full details: see the Privacy Policy.
