# Privacy Policy (Simple, Human‑Readable)

Last updated: 2025-08-24

## Who we are
SkillBridge is a learner-to-learner skill exchange app. We help people find and coordinate help without using money.

## What we collect
- Account info: email, display name, language, theme preference
- Location: precise GPS for matching (required). We only show others an approximate area
- Profile: your short “What I can provide” summary, optional bio
- Trust signals: endorsements, mandatory post-exchange ratings (stars, tags, comment)
- Messages: end-to-end encrypted (E2EE). We cannot read your messages
- Media: optional voice notes and intros (encrypted at rest; intro viewing requires consent)
- Device data: app version, basic diagnostics (no ad IDs)

### Full data map (who can see what)
- You only: full profile, exact GPS, messages plaintext on your device
- Match candidates and matches: pseudonym, languages, availability, trust summary, approximate area; intros only after consent
- Admins: no access to message content; limited access to metadata for safety/abuse handling
- Service providers (processors): infrastructure (cloud hosting), notifications, optional Google Calendar. No secondary use

## How we use it
- Match you with relevant people near you
- Power trust and reputation (completion, ratings, endorsements)
- Deliver features you choose (chat, scheduling, calendar sync)
- Improve the app safely (aggregate analytics, no personal profiling)

### Legal bases (where applicable)
- Contract: to provide the service features you request
- Legitimate interests: fraud prevention, service security, minimal analytics
- Consent: notifications, intros viewing, Google Calendar connection

## What we don’t do
- No public user directory
- No sale of your data
- No ads-based tracking
- No reading of your messages (E2EE)

## Sharing
- With other users: only what’s needed to connect (pseudonym, languages, availability, approximate area, trust summary). Real name/photo are off by default; you can opt in
- With service providers: cloud hosting, push notifications, optional Google Calendar. They must follow strict data protection obligations
- With authorities: only if legally required

### Third parties we use
- Hosting and database: Google Cloud / Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Functions)
- Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging
- Calendar (optional): Google Calendar

## Security
- Messages use Signal‑compatible E2EE (X3DH + Double Ratchet)
- Media is encrypted at rest; access is permission‑checked
- OAuth tokens (e.g., Google Calendar) are stored encrypted server-side

### Additional safeguards
- Role-based access, least privilege
- Rate limits on sensitive endpoints (e.g., location updates)
- Audit logs for consented intro access

## Your controls
- Language, theme, privacy toggles (real name/photo)
- Manage intros and endorsements (consent and visibility)
- Download or delete your data. Deleting your account removes personal data where feasible and severs identifiers from remaining aggregates

### Your rights (where laws apply)
- Access your data, correct it, delete it
- Object to certain processing; withdraw consent anytime
- Port your data where technically feasible

## Data retention
- Messages: you can delete; old items may auto-trim after a period
- Logs: minimal and rotated
- Legal or abuse-related holds may apply where required

Detailed retention table (defaults)
- Accounts: while active; 30 days after deletion request for recovery, then purge
- Messages: user-controlled; optional auto-trim after N days (configurable)
- Ratings/endorsements: kept to preserve trust history unless you delete your account
- Calendar tokens: removed immediately on disconnect

## Kids
This app is for people 16+ (or the age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are below the minimum age.

## Changes
We’ll post updates here and, if changes are significant, notify you in the app.
